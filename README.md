## 🎮 Why Games? Why EA?

I've been analyzing EA franchises (FIFA → EA FC, F1, The Sims) to understand how design decisions impact player retention and monetization. What fascinates me is the **data behind the product** — how a progression tweak changes Day 7 retention by 8%, or how a battle pass redesign shifts ARPU by 15%.

Live-service games are the most metrics-driven products I've seen. I want to be in that world at EA.

## 📂 Portfolio Contents

### **Case Studies (3 Real-World Projects)**

#### **1. Player Retention Analysis**
📄 [01-RETENTION-ANALYSIS.md](./01-RETENTION-ANALYSIS.md)

Identifying early churn patterns in live games. **Key Finding:** 45% of new players churn by Day 3 (tutorial abandonment). Recommendations include streamlined onboarding and Day 2-3 re-engagement campaigns.

**Impact:** +$120K annually through retention improvements  
**Skills:** SQL, Python (cohort analysis), statistical insights, data visualization  

💻 [View Code](./code/retention_analysis.py)

---

#### **2. A/B Test: Battle Pass Price Optimization**
📄 [02-AB-TEST-ANALYSIS.md](./02-AB-TEST-ANALYSIS.md)

Designed and analyzed an A/B test to optimize Battle Pass pricing ($9.99 → $7.99). **Results:** 28% conversion lift (p < 0.001, statistically significant).

**Impact:** +$186K annual revenue  
**Skills:** Experimental design, power analysis, hypothesis testing, statistical significance, confidence intervals  

💻 [View Code](./code/ab_test_analysis.py)

---

#### **3. Game Health Monitoring Dashboard**
📄 [03-GAME-HEALTH-DASHBOARD.md](./03-GAME-HEALTH-DASHBOARD.md)

Real-time operational analytics for live games. Tracks DAU/MAU, retention cohorts, feature adoption, revenue health, and system performance.

**Impact:** Reduced incident detection time from 3 days → 24 hours  
**Skills:** Data pipeline architecture, SQL, Python ETL, BI dashboard design (Tableau/PowerBI)
